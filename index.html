Problems We Solved

Traditional smartwatches suffer from chronic energy limitations. SOLARA redefines the paradigm.

Frequent Charging
Daily charging rituals tether users to cables, outlets, and chargers constantly.
Battery Degradation
Repeated charge cycles damage Li-Po cells, reducing capacity over time.

Electricity-hungry devices contribute to growing carbon footprints globally.
✅
Our Solutions

Flexible photovoltaic cells convert ambient sunlight to electricity in real time.

Solar-powered operation produces zero carbon emissions during use.
03 — System Architecture

Hardware Components

Hover over each component to explore its technical specifications.

🌞
Flexible Solar Panel
GaAs Thin-Film · 80mm²
Monocrystalline gallium arsenide cells achieve 28% efficiency. Integrated into the watch bezel using a 0.3mm flexible substrate. Operates in 10,000–100,000 lux range.
🔌
ESP32-S3 MCU
Xtensa LX7 · 240MHz · BLE 5.0
Dual-core 240MHz processor with 8MB PSRAM. Handles charging logic, BLE communication, sensor fusion, and power management with ultra-low sleep current of 10µA.
🔋
Li-Po Battery
3.7V · 450mAh · 1.665Wh
Slim 3.0mm polymer cell with integrated protection circuit. Supports 500+ full charge cycles. Charge cutoff at 4.2V; deep-discharge protection at 3.0V.
📡
Wireless Charging
Qi Standard · 5W · 15mm range
BQ51003 wireless power receiver with resonant coupling. Auto-activates when battery drops below threshold and solar input is below 0.2W minimum.
